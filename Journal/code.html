Calm Reflective Grateful

Evening Reflection

Today felt like a slow tide pulling away from the shore, leaving behind a clear, damp stretch of sand. I found myself noticing the smaller details—the way the light caught the edge of my coffee cup, the quiet hum of the heater, the rhythmic breathing of the city outside my window. There is a specific kind of peace that comes when you stop trying to manage the chaos and simply exist within it.

I am learning that reflection isn't just about looking back; it's about seeing where I am standing right now. I feel grounded, though perhaps a bit tired from the week's demands. It is enough to be here, in this quiet room, with these words.
