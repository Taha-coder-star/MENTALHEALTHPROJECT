# MindEase — Mental Health Support App

A quiet place for the mind, built for university students, 
between classes and everything else.

## About

MindEase is a high-fidelity mobile app prototype designed to 
address the mental health needs of university students at 
FAST-NUCES Karachi. The project was developed as part of the 
Technical and Business Writing course under the supervision 
of Ms. Nazia Imam.

The prototype was informed by a survey of 19 university 
students, which revealed that 94.7% experience stress yet 
89.5% use no mental health app. MindEase bridges this gap 
through a culturally relevant, anonymous-first, and 
visually simple mobile experience.

## Key Features

- Anonymous login — no personal data required
- Daily mood check-in with culturally local tags
- Private journal with mood and date context
- Box breathing exercise with visual animation
- Quranic verse and motivational quote of the day
- Resource library categorized by student stressors
- Anonymous community circle for peer support
- Progress tracker and gentle reminders

## Design System

- Colors: Mist #EEF2F7 · Sky #A8C5DA · Deepwater #5E89A8
- Typography: Times New Roman (headings) · Inter (UI)
- Style: Flat, calm, minimal — no gradients or textures
- Screens: 9 high-fidelity screens

## Research Summary

- Sample: 19 FAST-NUCES students
- Top stressor: Career uncertainty (84%)
- 89.5% currently use no mental health app
- 100% said easy UI is extremely important
- 42% prefer fully anonymous experience

## Tech Stack

- Design Tool: Stitch / Figma
- Research: Google Forms
- Documentation: LaTeX / Overleaf
